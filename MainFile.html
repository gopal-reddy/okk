Gayatri Vidya Parishad

The GVP College of Engineering, Visakhapatnam started in 1996,
has very quickly established itself as one of the most-preferred
private engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh. College has been
accredited by NAAC in 2009 with an A grade (with a score of 3.47/4.00).

The college has been sanctioned “Autonomous” status by the UGC and
conferred by the affiliating University, JNT University, Kakinada in 2009.
GVP College of Engineering has 7 UG Programs, 13 PG Programs and 8 Departments
were recognized as Research Centers by the affiliated University, JNTU-K,
Kakinada. All the 7 UG programmes are accredited by NBA and 5 of them are
accredited as per Outcome Based Education (OBE) and 5 PG programs are applied
for NBA as per OBE.
